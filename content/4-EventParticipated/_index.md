During my internship, I participated in three events organized by AWS Vietnam and the AWS First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) community. Each event connected classroom learning with industry perspectives on cloud architecture, AI, and career development.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)  
&emsp;**Event name:** AWS Enterprise Cloud Architectures & Industry Application — Swinburne Vietnam Study Tour  

&emsp;**Date:** 2026 (third annual Swinburne–AWS study tour)  

&emsp;**Location:** Amazon Web Services Vietnam  

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee  

&emsp;**Summary:** Study tour with Swinburne Vietnam students (including Cần Thơ campus) featuring AWS, Renova Cloud, and Cloud Kinetics speakers on cloud careers, data engineering in production, soft skills, and responsible AI use.

### [Event 2](4.2-Event2/)

&emsp;**Date & time:** 23 May 2026
